Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a long, thin, cylindrical pasta. It is a staple food of traditional Italian cuisine and can be served with various sauces and toppings.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© the garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the canned tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Toss the spaghetti with the sauce and serve hot, garnished with fresh basil.
