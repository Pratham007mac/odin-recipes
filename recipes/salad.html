Salad

Description

This salad is a refreshing mix of greens, vegetables, and a tangy dressing. It's a great side dish or a light meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of lettuce
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • Olive oil and vinegar for dressing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Chop all the vegetables and place them in a large bowl.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and vinegar, and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Toss everything together and serve immediately.
